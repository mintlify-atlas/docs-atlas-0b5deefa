Whether you're a student managing a capstone project, a professor supervising coursework, or an administrator overseeing institutional initiatives, PROJEIC gives your team the tools to plan, track, and deliver — all in one place.

## What PROJEIC offers

PROJEIC is purpose-built for academic engineering environments. It supports multiple project management methodologies, role-based access for diverse stakeholders, and public project visibility for the wider university community.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit the PROJEIC login page and register with your institutional email address. See [Authentication](/authentication) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    From the dashboard, click **New Project**, choose your methodology (Kanban, Scrum, or Scrumban), and configure visibility and status. See [Projects overview](/projects/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your team">
    Invite collaborators and assign roles — Admin, Supervisor, Student, or External. See [Members and roles](/projects/members-and-roles).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start tracking work">
    Create boards, add tasks, and begin your first sprint or Kanban workflow. See [Task management](/tasks/boards).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Public projects on PROJEIC are visible to anyone — no login required. Share your project URL with the broader EIC community to showcase your work.
</Note>
