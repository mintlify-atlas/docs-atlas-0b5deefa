PROJEIC is the project management platform for the Escuela de Ingeniería Coquimbo (EIC). Students use it to track capstone and coursework projects, professors supervise and review team progress, and administrators maintain institutional visibility across every active initiative — all from a single platform.

<Note>
  PROJEIC is purpose-built for the EIC community. Access and features are designed around the academic workflows of the Escuela de Ingeniería Coquimbo.
</Note>

## What PROJEIC offers

PROJEIC is built around the rhythms and needs of the EIC community. It supports multiple project management methodologies, role-based access for every type of stakeholder, and public project pages so the wider university community can follow progress without logging in.
