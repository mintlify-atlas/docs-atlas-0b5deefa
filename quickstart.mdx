---
title: "Get started with PROJEIC: account, project, and tasks"
description: "Create your PROJEIC account, configure your first project with a methodology and visibility setting, add team members, and assign your first task."
---

This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a new account to an active project with team members and tasks. By the end, you will have a working board ready for your team to use.

<Tip>
  Choose your methodology before creating a project. **Kanban** works best for continuous workflows with no fixed delivery dates. **Scrum** suits teams that want structured sprints with defined start and end dates. **Scrumban** is a hybrid that combines sprint planning with flexible Kanban-style columns.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register or log in">
    Go to `/auth/login` in your browser. If you already have an account, enter your email and password and click **Log in**. If you are new to PROJEIC, click the **Register** tab, fill in your name, email, and password, then submit the form.

    After a successful login or registration, PROJEIC stores your access token and user data in your browser's `localStorage` and redirects you to the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first project">
    From the dashboard, click **New Project**. Fill in the following fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | Name | A short, descriptive project title |
    | Description | What the project is about and its goals |
    | Methodology | `KANBAN`, `SCRUM`, or `SCRUMBAN` |
    | Visibility | `Public` (visible without login) or `Private` |

    Click **Create** to save. Your project is now active and ready for team members.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add team members">
    Open your project and go to the **Members** tab. Click **Invite member**, enter the person's email address, and choose their role:

    - **Admin** ŌĆö full project control, including settings and member management
    - **Supervisor** ŌĆö can review tasks and results but cannot change project settings
    - **Student** ŌĆö can create and update tasks assigned to them
    - **External** ŌĆö read-only access for stakeholders outside the EIC

    The invited person receives a notification and can accept the invitation from their notifications panel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your board">
    How you set up your board depends on the methodology you chose:

    **Kanban** ŌĆö go to the **Board** tab and click **Add column**. Create columns that represent the stages of your workflow, such as `To do`, `In progress`, and `Done`.

    **Scrum** ŌĆö go to the **Sprints** tab and click **New sprint**. Set a name, start date, and end date for your first sprint. Tasks added to the sprint appear on the sprint board.

    **Scrumban** ŌĆö create columns like Kanban, then assign tasks to sprints for planning purposes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first task">
    Inside your board or sprint, click **Add task** (or the `+` button in a column). Fill in:

    - **Title** ŌĆö a concise description of the work
    - **Description** ŌĆö additional context or acceptance criteria
    - **Priority** ŌĆö `Low`, `Medium`, `High`, or `Critical`
    - **Assignee** ŌĆö a team member from your project
    - **Due date** ŌĆö the date the task should be completed

    Save the task. It appears in your board column and is visible to all project members.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Projects set to **Public** visibility are accessible at `/proyectos` without any login. Anyone can view the project details, members, and progress ŌĆö useful for sharing work with the wider EIC community or external reviewers.
</Note>
